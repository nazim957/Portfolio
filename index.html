The main features are:

  • As a user, I want to utilize TextCraft as a word counter, enabling me to accurately gauge the number of words in my text.
  • As a user, I need TextCraft to serve as a character counting utility, allowing me to efficiently determine the number of characters present in my text.
  • As a user, I want to remove extra spaces from my text using TextCraft, ensuring a clean and tidy presentation.
  • As a user, I expect TextCraft to provide functionality for copying the manipulated text, enabling easy transfer to other documents or platforms.
